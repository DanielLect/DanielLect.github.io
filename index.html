MEET GEOFF (and Auric)

Geoff was born in Seattle, moved to Germany when he was young and then spent most of his teenage years in Arizona. He is a die-hard CARDINALS fan and enjoys the football banter that occurs every year with his friends that are Seahawks fans. He spent 20 years in the music industry as a Promoter, Stage and Production Manager, Boooking Agent, Event Coordinator and Stage Technician. As a promotor, Geoff has produced, overseen, managed and "pulled off" over 3000 shows and events in 3 different states, in over 50 different venues and has worked with thousands of Artists. In fact, that is the primary reason he chose the name "The Metal Shop" for his business since his primary focus as a promoter was heavy metal and hard rock bands. Now he enjoys working with other "heavy metals" along with Auric, his best friend. Auric is a Golden Retriever / Great Pyrenees mix that loves greeting customers when they arrive. His name means: of, relating to, or derived from Gold. It is also the first name of James Bond villian, Goldfinger. Auric is almost 4 years old (as of Aug. 2021) and has been helping out in the business since he was a puppy. He always enjoys making new friends and would love to meet YOU!
