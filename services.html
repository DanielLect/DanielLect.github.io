Whether you're buying to diversity, help protect your assets, or both, there are a number of different precious metals options available.

Private Mint Bullion:

This will generally be the lowest premium purchase option. Bullion coins and bars are minted by private mints worldwide carry no monetary designation. They simply trade and are valued based on their weight of precious metal content.

Government (sovereign) Coins:

Sovereign coins are minted by governments worldwide and carry a monetary value separate from the value of the precious metal content. They are fully backed by the nation minting them.

Numismatic Coins:

These are generally government minted coins that are valued based on desirable design, low mintage numbers and condition. The numismatic value (collectability) is assessed in addition to the value ofthe content metal.
